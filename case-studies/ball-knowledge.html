Project Preview

Ball Knowledge is a multiplayer trivia app designed for football enthusiasts. Players compete in real-time matches, testing their knowledge of players, clubs, statistics, and football history.

This project showcases my end-to-end product design process, from initial user research and competitive analysis through wireframing, prototyping, and high-fidelity UI design in Figma.

What to Expect

UX Research

User interviews with football fans, competitive analysis of existing trivia apps, and persona development to understand target users.

User Flows

Detailed user journey maps showing how players navigate through matchmaking, gameplay, and social features.

Wireframes

Low and mid-fidelity wireframes exploring different layouts and interaction patterns for the core game experience.

High-Fidelity Mockups

Polished Figma designs with a complete design system, including typography, colors, components, and interactive prototypes.
